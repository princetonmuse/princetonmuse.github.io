What if conversation were more than just small talk? Become part of a forum for sincere, meaningful exchange.

Learn more

Hello, we're Muse

We are an organization that offers an escape from the regular small talk that inhabits the Orange Bubble. By providing a carefully selected menu of conversation questions, we hope to facilitate interesting and genuine conversation between two strangers.

Based on an original idea by Oxford scholar Theodore Zeldin, Princeton Muse seeks to promote openness and curiosity through a sincere exchange between two people, away from judgment and convention.

To learn more about Muse, we encourage you to visit the site of our "parent" chapter, The Oxford Muse Society, and the Oxford Muse Foundation. You can also read about us in The Daily Princetonian.

Events

How do I join?

Princeton Students

Come to our monthly events—all undergrads & grad students are welcome. Hear about the next one by joining our listserv or liking us on Facebook! We also encourage you to reach out if you're interested in helping plan events, or have any suggestions.

Beyond Princeton

Start your own Muse! As the first Muse chapter in the US, we're eager to grow Muse's presence on other campuses. If you'd like to bring the Muse experience to your university, please contact us for more information.
